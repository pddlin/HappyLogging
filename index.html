Loggerithm

a cozy lumberjack math game — chop · count · craft

desktop: WASD + Space · mobile: on-screen pad · Esc for menu
📖

How to play

A hush of pines and lantern-light — reindeer graze, lions prowl the ridge, and birds wheel over the lake.

🌲
Chop trees to drop logs — each carries a number you can see before you swing.
🪵
Collect logs by walking over them. They stack in your bar below.
✦
Craft at the workbench: stand at it, tap two logs, then pick + − × — the result you want becomes a new log.
🎯
Make a log equal to the target to clear the grove.
🦁
Avoid the lions! Touch one and the pride chases you out — you restart this grove.
